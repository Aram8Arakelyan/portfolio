---
title: 'Why I write in public'
description: 'A short note on keeping a blog, and what I hope to get out of writing things down.'
date: '2026-06-26'
tags: ['writing', 'career']
---

Writing forces clarity. When an idea only lives in my head it can stay comfortably
vague — but the moment I try to explain it to someone else, the gaps show up.

That is the main reason this blog exists. A few more:

- **It compounds.** Notes I write today become references for future me.
- **It invites feedback.** Being wrong in public is the fastest way to learn.
- **It is a record.** A year of posts is a surprisingly honest changelog of how
  my thinking has shifted.

I am not aiming for a fixed schedule. The goal is simply to write things down when
they feel worth sharing — short notes, longer deep-dives, and everything in between.

Thanks for reading.
